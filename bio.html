Namitala Dorothy Shivan®


Praise God our Dear Lord Almighty. Of course my name is Namitala Dorothy Shivan aka Dorathie. I am devoted to Christ and serve a mighty living God. May the Almighty stay great and merciful. Every Sunday I go to church for my prayers. I love outing, touring and adventuring. I have travelled to many paces across Uganda, these include some; One Love Beach Busabala, Victoria Lake and many more.




Home town


My home town is Kawanda, Kamapala district. I love this place so much because my family is settling here. My family is not willing to transfer from Kawanda right now. This place is perfect for our stay. We are next to town, so things are always simple and cheap to purchase. With this we are not willing to transfer right now. I love my family so much. I give my siblings time and care for them a lot.



Friendships


All of my life I've had friends on my side and supporting me always. I love all of my loved ones most especially my mother, I love her the most with my relatives follwing and then my friends. A great hug to all of those friends at Namirembe Hill side secondary who have supported me when at school, and those friends(OGs and OBs) who supported me at primary level. But all in all, I thank the Lord Almghty God for blessing me with my sweet caring family. I pray that my family stays together and never to depart in Christ I've prayed, Amen.



Media


Well media is like I am addicted to it because I always have to post my new lifetime experiences and nice funny moments. I can't believe I tend to stay on media platforms such as Instagram and WhatsApp. All these keep me awake, but because of the Lord's grace I manage to control myself and go to sleep, Amen.



Games


Games to my side, I like simple games such as smart phone games. And I play responsibly because I have to spare some time for doing home chores. I spare less time in games most of the time in travelling, touring, praying to God. I put God first in everything I do because he has been here for me since my childhood up to date. Amen



Fun


Fun to me is like having my phone at hand and making jokes with my siblings. Laughing with my mother in the kitchen, dining room, sitting room and green garden. Enjoying meals with my siblings together as a family. Always my heart is filled with fun and joy when with my family and God, I am next to my Lord always. Those are my fun areas. And without my family, I can't have fun and happiness.
It's believed that fun begins with someone but mine begins with my family and God. Amen



Moods


My moods go high when I hear that we going for an outing travel. For me, mood swings up because of my joy and happiness inside my heart brought by Lord Almighty.



Music


My favourite music is Gospel only because I love God my Almighty. Circular music is not my favourite at all for it's against Christian faith and anything against Christian faith is not part of my life. I am selective at the music I choose to listen to all times, and this has helped me stay focussed to the Lord God our Almighty.



Fashion


My fashion is just outfits for great Sunday prayers and outing casuals. I am more in loving fashion and much in living fashion. I am perfect in fashion and I can make it out for myself for the grace of the Lord Almighty.



Technology


Well I like technology but not much because I only have my handset for chatting, playing music, gaming, checking on my platforms, snapping, playing gospel music and many more. All in all the Lord has anabled me to have all this!!!

I love going to the beach and having sand castles built with brothers and sisters alongside my mother watching us play. Fantasy

Space

Love


I love my God, family, best friend and friends. I am already a friend to God so being a friend to the rest like my friends is just an addition to the love for God. God wants me to continue loving my family and friends as I love myself. I try hard to show the love by smiling to my beloved ones including my family, best friend and friends. The Lord is still on my side, Amen.



Relaxation


I always feel relaxed when I open my Bible to read some verses recorded during Sunday services at Church and even during fellowships at home. Praying in the day and night makes my heart feel relaxed and free from bad thoughts that might be encoutered by my brain. Thank you Lord.
